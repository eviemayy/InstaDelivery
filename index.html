InstaDelivery

InstaDelivery is an online delivery service attached to a grocery store to help customers and the store safely make purchases and deliver goods during the age of COVID-19.

Shop - Add, update, delete products to/from an order. Place an order.

Orders - Update/Delete an order.

Payment - Add a payment, update/delete a payment.

Deliverer - Assign/Remove orders to a deliverer.

Sign Up - Assign/Remove/Update customers and deliverers
